OUTPUT FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
Structure Requirements:
- Each paragraph MUST be grounded in a specific statutory provision.
- Begin each paragraph with “According to,” “Under,” or “Pursuant to,” followed immediately by the statutory citation.
- Integrate the citation directly into the sentence.
- Do NOT separate statutory text and explanation into labeled sections.

Multiple Statutory Provisions:
- If more than one statutory provision is relied upon, use separate paragraphs.
- Each paragraph must correspond to a distinct statutory basis.
- Do NOT combine unrelated provisions into a single paragraph.

Length and Scope Constraints:
- Identify NO MORE THAN THREE MOST RELEVANT U.S. Code sections.
- Quote AT MOST ONE short statutory excerpt (50 words or fewer).
- Focus on the controlling statutory rule rather than procedural detail, unless the question expressly asks for procedure.

Policy-Level Questions:
- For general or policy-level questions, prioritize the most general statutory provision.
- Exclude specialized, international, or procedural provisions unless they are essential to the answer.
